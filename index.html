music Girls who code is a in school club at Erwin Middle School that girls made a website for pet owners and made an app for students struggling with mental health issues.

On the abuse page you can see how animals that are abused have a hard time trying to survive. You can also see how to take care of abused animals you find. You can also learn about what they do to the animals that are badly abused.

On caring for you pet you can see how to help your sick or hurt pet or stray animals. You can also find vets and animal hospitals that can help you with the situation.

On Asheville shelters you can find shelters in you area and see what animals they have for you to adopt.
